OUR STORY

From Warrior Strategy to a Guide's Heart

Phaelix AI was born from a journey of purpose. It started with the founder's last name, Guerra (war), and a mission to help businesses fight inefficiency. But a challenge in securing a domain led to a moment of clarity—a "Phoenix moment."

The true mission wasn't about the fight, but the victory. The name Phaelix was created, combining Phae- (Greek for 'light'), Felix (Latin for 'success'), and the founder's own lifelong nickname, Phael (from Raphael), which represents guidance and healing.

"We bring the strategy of a warrior (Guerra) and the heart of a guide (Phael) to ensure your success (Felix)."
